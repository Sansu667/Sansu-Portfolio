About Me

I am a philosophy student at the National University of Colombia, where I explore the complexities of critical thinking and reflection. My passion for programming has led me to develop projects in Python and Javascript, tools I use to bring my ideas to life.

I have collaborated with an educational institution with the goal of preserving the memory of a neighborhood, creating programs that foster connection and remembrance of our local history.
